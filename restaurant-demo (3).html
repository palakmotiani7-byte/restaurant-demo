Our Story

A Kitchen with
Nothing to Hide

Born from a simple belief — great food has nothing to hide. Our open kitchen lets you watch every dish come to life, from the moment spices hit the tawa to the final garnish.

Nestled at Panorama Circle, Ramnagariya, Dhaba Stories is Jagatpura's go-to for families, celebrations, and quiet evening meals. We blend the warmth of a Rajasthani home kitchen with the finesse of modern presentation.
